:::caution
By deleting your account, you will **no longer be eligible to gain WXM rewards**, and you will **no longer be able to view your station data** in the WeatherXM apps.

This **will delete** the following data:
 - Your name.
 - Your email address.
 - Your station(s) details, their location, and their token rewards history.

This **will not delete**:
 - The weather data you’ve shared with the network.
 - The WXM rewards you’ve collected. These are yours forever!

#### We do not collect nor store any other personal data!

If you have any questions regarding your data and the deletion of your account, please contact our  [support team](https://support.weatherxm.com)
:::
