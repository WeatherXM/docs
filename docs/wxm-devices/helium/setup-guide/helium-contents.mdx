The box includes all the necessary parts that your new weather station needs in order to work properly.

Included in the box is the:
1. Outdoor weather station
1. M5 weather station gateway
1. Wind cup assembly
1. Wind vane
1. Two U-bolts, 4 nuts, 1 metallic plate, small wrench
1. USB cable
1. GPS antenna
1. RF antenna

## Outdoor sensor

The outdoor sensor is a set of sensors encased in a plastic shell that ensures the proper function of the weather station.

The outdoor sensor is responsible for:

1. the collection of measurements of local temperature, humidity, rain, wind speed and wind direction, and
1. the wireless transmission of the data to the weather miner.

This part of your weather station is placed outdoors.

For further details about an advisable installation of the outdoor sensor, please see [Setting up the Weather Station](/wxm-devices/wifi-m5-bundle/setup-weather-station).

1. Rain collector

1. Solar panel
1. Antenna
1. Light sensor
1. U-Bolt
1. Battery compartment
1. Reset button
1. LED Indicator: light on for 4s on powers up. Then the LED will flash once every 16 seconds (the sensor transmission update period).

## M5 Miner

This is the device responsible for receiving weather measurements from the outdoor sensor and transmitting them via WiFi to IPFS and the WeatherXM IoT platform.

The weather miner is advised to be placed indoors, in an environment of relatively constant temperature (ideally within a daily range of <4Â°C for proper functioning of the barometer),
near a window (for better GPS signal). The weather miner should be permanently connected to power and activated in order to be able to receive, record and upload measurements to the internet.
