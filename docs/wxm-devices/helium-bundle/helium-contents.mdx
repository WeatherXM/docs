## Not Included

You would also need:
1. Batteries  1.5V **not rechargeable** (x6 AA)
1. Aluminium pole (recommended 60mm pole diameter & at least 2m height)
1. Tools and parts to secure the pole

:::danger 
If you use rechargeable batteries, you may harm your device
:::
